## 1. Las Vegas Raiders — Fernando Mendoza, QB, Indiana

**6'5" | 225 lbs**

Mendoza is the best QB in this class and has been set in stone as the #1 pick since the season has ended. He will need time to develop but he can already diagnose defenses extremely fast and make any throw. He is mobile enough to navigate the pocket and use his legs when he needs to. Great character and work ethic.
